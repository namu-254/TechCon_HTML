Our History

TechCon began as a small gathering of innovators and technology enthusiasts in 2010. Over the years, it has grown into one of the most influential technology conferences in Africa, attracting global speakers, startups, and tech leaders.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 aims to inspire technological innovation, connect professionals, and empower young creators to shape the digital future. Our mission is to create a collaborative space where ideas thrive.
